Butter Chicken Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- Salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, pureed
- 1/4 cup cream
- Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
2. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© the chopped onions until golden brown.
3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's no longer pink.
4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
5. Stir in the cream and simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.
6. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.

Enjoy your delicious Butter Chicken!
